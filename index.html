WHAT IS PSYCH ENGINE ULTRA?

ONLINE SYSTEM

Login system, global leaderboard and cloud save. Register once on the website or in-game, compete worldwide. Your scores sync automatically after every song you complete.

ANDROID SUPPORT

Fully optimized Android port with touch controls and performance improvements. Play anywhere, anytime — same account, same leaderboard across all your devices.

PERFORMANCE BOOST

Improved memory usage, faster loading times and optimized rendering. Built on Psych Engine with full Lua & HScript mod support, enhanced for Ultra.

// FAQ
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
What is Psych Engine Ultra?
GENERAL▶
Psych Engine Ultra is an enhanced fork of Psych Engine — the popular Friday Night Funkin' mod engine. Ultra adds online features like a global leaderboard, account system, level progression, and more on top of the solid Psych Engine foundation.
What does "Ultra" add over regular Psych Engine?
FEATURES▶
Ultra adds: Online account registration & login (web + in-game), Global real-time leaderboard, Level & XP system based on total score, Automatic score submission after every song, Country selection & profile stats, and a fully optimized Android build.
How do I create an account?
ACCOUNT▶
You can register right here on the website by clicking REGISTER in the top right corner. You can also create an account in-game when you first launch. Once registered, you can log in on any platform using the same credentials.
Is an account required to play?
ACCOUNT▶
No! You can skip account creation and play offline. However, without an account your scores won't appear on the global leaderboard and you won't have a level or profile. You can always create an account later.
How does the level system work?
GAMEPLAY▶
Every song you complete adds to your total score. Your level is calculated using: Level = √(TotalScore / 1000). Only your best score per song per difficulty counts toward the total, so replaying songs can improve your score.
Is Psych Engine Ultra free?
GENERAL▶
Yes, completely free and open source. No ads, no paywalls, no premium features. Everything is available to everyone. The source code is on GitHub and you're free to fork and modify it for your own projects.
Can I use mods with Ultra?
MODS▶
Yes! Psych Engine Ultra is fully compatible with Lua and HScript mods, just like regular Psych Engine. Place your mods in the /mods folder as usual. Note: online scores are only tracked for songs from the base game.
I forgot my password. What do I do?
ACCOUNT▶
Click SIGN IN on the website, enter your email in the username field, and click "FORGOT PASSWORD". A reset link will be sent to your email within a few minutes. In-game, enter your email in the username field and tap the forgot password button.
